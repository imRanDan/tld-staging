Frequently Asked Questions

Why Stage?

Staging is all about maximizing your profits and minimizing time on market.

Staged homes spend 73% less time on the market, allowing you to sell faster than other non-staged properties. Even in the current seller's market, buyers appreciate a great first impression and are willing to pay more for it. Staging your home highlights its best features, masks it's "could be better" spots and increases its livability potential during showings.

Which areas do we service?

We have happy clients within the GTA. Halton, and Hamilton regions.

Our process?

We have a five-step process inclusive of the following:

  1. Staging Consultation: Each house is unique and we will meet to discuss what plan works best for you to get the best ROI on your property.
  2. Staging Strategy: Specific recommendations to bring out the best in your property.
  3. Proposal: An outline of project costs and timelines.
  4. Staging/Showcasing: Setup and execution of the staging strategy.
  5. De-staging: Removal of all staging accessories and furniture.
